The hard part? Getting 10 teams to agree on what "success" means.

## The Logic

- **Clicks don't matter** if nobody converts.
- **Revenue doesn't matter** if users churn.
- **Uptime doesn't matter** if nobody shows up.

But DAU-to-goal? Everyone can move that needle.

## Key Takeaways

1. **The best metric ends arguments.** If teams are still debating what success looks like after you pick a metric, you picked the wrong one.

2. **Everyone has to be able to influence it.** A metric that only Product can move isn't a company metric.

3. **Delete before you add.** Cut ruthlessly until you have one number everyone believes in.

4. **Alignment beats velocity.** 10 teams moving fast in different directions is chaos. 10 teams moving together is momentum.
